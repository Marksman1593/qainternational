About Us

AIS-QAI, formerly known as Asian Inspection & Warehouse Services, became a pioneer after being founded in 1975. We have steadily advanced along the way to becoming a dominant force in the field of quality evaluation. Our humble beginnings in Pakistan have left a global imprint that has spread its impact to China and the US. However, our sphere of influence encompasses a wide range of areas, including South America, Europe, South Asia, and Southeast Asia.

At AIS-QAI, We achieve excellence through a wide range of comprehensive services. We provide Statistical management reports, Supplier report cards, providing insights to benefit businesses. Our purchasing procedures are comprehensive and include sourcing, certification, and careful quality control. Our approach to business is based on a strong commitment to preserving quality standards.

Our area of expertise includes extensive evaluation of a broad range of goods to guarantee outstanding quality in every aspect. Additionally, we are proficient at vigilantly tracking shipments to promote the timely and secure transportation of goods across international borders.

Over the years, AIS-QAI has transcended boundaries, serving not just as a service provider but also as a symbol of quality, safety, and compliance. Our journey is a reflection of our persistent dedication to excellence, driving businesses toward a future defined by unwavering quality and trust.

SERVICES AND SOLUTIONS

Factory Audits


It’s important to understand your supplier as well as you understand your product. An audit enables you to confidently choose a supplier that meets your standards for manufacturing and compliance. An audit can also ensure that a supplier would be a good partner long term. It’s a pivotal upfront investment that’s guaranteed pay for itself in savings many times over time.

Learn More

Inspection and Sampling services

We make sure your products are up to your customers’ specs and standards and clear industry and government regulations. We’re on-site when you can’t be, doing product checks, QC process factory documentation and on-site random sampling. You’ll know your brand and reputation are protected.

Learn More

Compliance & Performance Testing

Our compliance standards guarantee you’ll meet industry and government regulations. And testing in our own labs give us complete control over making sure you’ve got a high-quality product that meets expectations. Let us help you create a protocol your brand can stand behind.

Learn More

Fabric Monitoring & Materials Management

Fabric and materials QC comes with high customer expectations. We make sure your textiles are made right by monitoring every aspect of the production process at the factory level. We verify your fabric quality, performance, safety, and compliance with regulatory standards for your product’s destination.

Learn More

Compliance Consulting & Monitoring

Being compliant is becoming exponentially more difficult every year. If you don’t know where to start, or want help keeping up with the rules, we’ll work with you to scrutinize your product and identify the best path for its compliance requirements. For 30 years, we’ve been consulting with importers to build compliance programs for all types of products headed to the USA, Canada, EU, Aus/NZ, China, and elsewhere.

Learn More

Reporting & Statistical Data

We can help you catch defects and other signs of a problem before the shipment leaves the warehouse. Our web-based software system, Quality Port® gives you 24/7 access to your compliance and quality data. Its interactive tools and reports allow you to monitor, manage, and compile real-time data from any inspection report format, complete with photos, data, and quality compliance activities.
